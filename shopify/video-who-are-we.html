WHO ARE WE?


Founded in St. John’s, Newfoundland & Labrador, Studio VERSŌ is an independent movement studio redefining the fitness experience. Our forward-thinking approach to wellness is now available wherever you are, through gear, apparel, and tools designed to move with you.


Rooted in music, movement, and meaningful connection, our products reflect the same energy you’ll find in our studios — a culture built on clarity, focus, and self-expression.


We’re proud to foster a community grounded in diversity, inclusion, equity, and accountability. Whether you're stepping into the studio or showing up from home, we’re here to support your journey to a happier, healthier, more connected life.
